Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this destive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas
FEATURED IN: Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon blasamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not neccessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized -mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachement, mix together all the dough ingrediants until the flour is fully absorded. You don't need to acctually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
